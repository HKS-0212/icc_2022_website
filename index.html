Timeline

History of our club

  • FOUNDING

    2009

    Founded, in 2009, as Genese-de-Strategie with the primary objective to edify students about the world of business strategies by Mr. Shailesh Gupta, Mr. Harsh Gupta and Mr. Sudipto Sannigrahi along with Ms. Srichandana Nagoji, Business Club has come a long way to be the business hub on campus and now boasts of a team comprising more than 50 highly driven dynamic individuals handpicked from the student population of IIT-Kharagpur

  • INDIAN CASE CHALLENGE (ICC)

    2012

    Indian Case Challenge, first national level competition organised by Business Club was started off in this year.

  • INDIAN BIZ CHALLENGE

    2015

    Indian Business Challenge was started off as Business Club’s first B-plan competition in this year. It’s third edition, Indian Case Challenge 2014 was a spectacular success.


    AFFILIATION UNDER TSG

    Affiliation under the Technology Students Gymkhana (TSG), IIT Kharagpur has been a milestone in the club's journey.

  • FINANCIAL LITERACY DRIVE

    2015

    The club’s first Financial Literacy Drive was conducted in this year.

  • PARTNERSHIP WITH CIMA

    2015

    The fourth edition of the Indian Case Challenge was a huge success. We also had the privilege to partner with CIMA (Chartered Institute of Management Accountants) this time.


    CASE-A-THON

    Business Club also collaborated with ZS Associates to organise a workshop on Consulting followed by an overnight Case-a-thon

  • G-BAC (GLOBAL BUSINESS ANALYTICS CHALLENGE)

    2017

    Business Club, in a triangular partnership with IIM-Ahmedabad and SRCC, Delhi organized the first Global Business Analytics Challenge in the Global Millennium Summit. The Summit,held at Dubai, brought together students from the world’s leading universities and notable leaders from fields of governance, business, technology and academia .


    BIZ - QUIZ

    A new event “Biz Quiz” was also introduced along with the fifth edition of ICC for the first time which was aimed at quenching the thirsts of quizzards with questions from myriad range of topics pertaining to corporate and business world.


    PARTNERSHIP WITH RBI

    An essay writing competition was organized and evaluated by the members of Business Club. The influence of fintech on increasing access in finance in India was a very relevant topic and a lot of innovative ideas were received.

  • PARTNERSHIP WITH IBM

    2018

    Business Club collaborated with the technology giant IBM’s black-box analytics tool SPSS to organize an awareness drive for data enthusiasts on campus. The event witnessed active participation of students from all backgrounds and eventually also served as a stepping stone of our healthy association with IBM.


    ART AND SCIENCE OF PROBLEM SOLVING


    Sales and Marketing Consulting giant, ZS Associates joined hands with Business Club to organize an interactive session for the aspiring consultants of IIT Kharagpur. The unique case-solving approaches shared during the session gathered wide appreciation and the overwhelming turnout ended up setting new benchmarks for the club.

Format

The event will be conducted in two stages


Stage 1: Prelims

  • The preliminary problem statement will be sent to the participating teams once they register on the portal.
  • Registrations will be open from 20th December 2021 to 6th January 2022, 11:59 p.m IST.
  • The final deadline for the preliminary case submissions is 7th January 2022, 11:59 p.m. IST, and all participants must abide by it.
  • The list of qualifying teams will be released through all of Business Club, IIT Kharagpur’s social media pages after which we shall move to Stage 2.

Stage 2: Finals

  • All the shortlisted teams shall receive the final case by email.
  • The finalists would be presenting their solution to the final case during Kshitij (18th -22nd January), 2022 via an online video conferencing platform.
  • Under no circumstances will a modification of team members be permitted, once the preliminary solution is submitted.

Rules


  • Online registration is mandatory for participation.


  • Each team must have at least 3 and at most 5 members


  • Students from different colleges can be in the same team.


  • Both undergraduate and postgraduate students are allowed to participate.


  • Teams must mention the names of the members, their respective colleges and contact details on the first page of the report.


  • Plagiarism will not be tolerated.


  • All decisions made by the judges and Business Club, IIT Kharagpur will be final and binding.

About ICC

The Indian Case Challenge hosted annually by Business Club, IIT Kharagpur, is India’s Largest Business Case Competition. The 10th Edition of the ICC is brought to you in association with Kshitij, Asia’s largest Techno-Management fest. ICC 2022 brings to table prizes of over 1.5 Lakhs at stake.

With the previous edition of ICC witnessing over 4000 outstation participants and teams from 9 international colleges, ICC 2022 is sure to provide you the opportunity to compete with the brightest business intellects across the globe.
