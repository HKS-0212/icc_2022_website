The 2022 Edition will be conducted in association with Kshitij,

About ICC

The Indian Case Challenge, popularly known as ICC, is organized every year by Business Club, IIT Kharagpur. ICC also gives you the platform to showcase your talent in business management and to hone your skills to become the business leaders of the future.

The Indian Case Challenge witnessed its seventh edition in 2019. The previous edition of ICC received registrations of over 450 teams for the preliminary round. The current edition promises more grandeur in terms of the quality of participation as well as the prize money.
